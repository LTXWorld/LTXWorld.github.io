## Xiaomi MMO

~~This is the newest LLM from XiaoMi,a Chinese moblie phone company,which also make a good deal in new energy car.~~

This is the lateset LLM released by XiaoMi,a prominent Chinese smartphone manufacturer that has also recently made significant ==strides== in the EV sector.

1. Testing for Over-Aggressive Safety Filters

First, I wanted to test whether the model suffers from "false refusal"—where safety ==guardrails== are so strict that they ==hinder== normal functionality.

![1](/img/54/sqlInjection.png)

As the results shows,the model refused to answer a standard SQL injection query.This confirms that the model is indeed **over-defensive**,==prioritizing safety to a fault==.
